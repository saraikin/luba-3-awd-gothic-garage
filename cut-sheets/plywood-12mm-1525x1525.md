# Plywood cut sheet — 12 mm, 1525×1525 mm sheets

Draft only. Verify real charging station and mower clearance before cutting.

## Target external size

- Length: 1200 mm
- Width: 1000 mm
- Structural wall height: 650–700 mm
- Visual height with roof/tower: 1300–1500 mm

## Sheet 1 — side walls

- Left side wall: 1200 × 700 mm
- Right side wall: 1200 × 700 mm

Optional:

- Cut decorative side-window openings later, after Stage 1 testing.
- Keep side walls rectangular for Stage 1.

## Sheet 2 — floor, ceiling, rear wall

- Floor/base: 1200 × 1000 mm
- Removable ceiling / compartment divider: approx. 1080 × 860 mm
- Rear wall: 1000 × 700 mm plus optional gable extension

Notes:

- Ceiling panel should be removable for service.
- Rear wall can be rectangular at Stage 1 and visually extended with decorative gable later.

## Sheet 3 — front wall and roof base

- Front wall: 1000 × 1100 mm with practical gate opening
- Roof left slope: approx. 1200 × 600 mm
- Roof right slope: approx. 1200 × 600 mm

Gate opening:

- Width: approx. 700 mm
- Height: approx. 420 mm

The Gothic arch can be decorative and larger than the actual gate opening.

## Additional plywood/offcuts

Use offcuts for:

- Internal shelves.
- Cable cover.
- Power supply mounting panel.
- False front gable.
- Tower base.
- Erker backing plates.

## Assembly note

Use 30×30 or 40×40 mm battens as the main structural joining method. Do not rely on tight plywood slots unless using CNC.
