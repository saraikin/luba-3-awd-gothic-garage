# Plywood cut sheet — 12 mm, 2500×1250 mm sheet

Draft only. Verify real charging station and mower clearance before cutting.

Store sheet format:

- 2500 × 1250 × 12 mm

## Design variants

This project should keep two size variants:

1. **Minimal variant** — close to the minimum shelter requirement.
2. **Comfort variant** — more service space and easier construction.

The robot does not need internal maneuvering space. It docks and undocks along one fixed trajectory. Keep about 1500 mm of straight, level, obstacle-free path in front of the charging station.

## Variant A — Minimal footprint

Target internal robot bay:

- 900 × 600 × 500 mm minimum internal clearance

Approximate external size with 12 mm plywood and batten frame:

- Length: 950–1000 mm
- Width: 650–700 mm
- Structural wall height: 550–600 mm
- Visual height with false floors/tower: 1200–1450 mm

### Suggested panels

- Floor/base: 1000 × 700 mm
- Left side wall: 1000 × 600 mm
- Right side wall: 1000 × 600 mm
- Rear wall: 700 × 600 mm plus optional gable extension
- Front wall: 700 × 900–1100 mm with practical gate opening
- Removable ceiling / storage divider: approx. 900 × 600 mm
- Roof left slope: approx. 1000 × 500–550 mm
- Roof right slope: approx. 1000 × 500–550 mm

Gate opening:

- Width: approx. 700 mm
- Height: approx. 420 mm

Note: with a 700 mm external front width, a 700 mm gate opening leaves little structural side material. For this reason, the minimal variant should use a reinforced front frame with timber battens and possibly a slightly wider external width, e.g. 750–800 mm.

## Variant B — Comfort footprint

Target internal robot bay:

- Approx. 1000–1080 × 700–860 × 600 mm

Approximate external size:

- Length: 1100–1200 mm
- Width: 800–1000 mm
- Structural wall height: 650–700 mm
- Visual height with false floors/tower: 1300–1500 mm

### Suggested panels

- Floor/base: 1200 × 1000 mm
- Left side wall: 1200 × 700 mm
- Right side wall: 1200 × 700 mm
- Rear wall: 1000 × 700 mm plus optional gable extension
- Front wall: 1000 × 1100 mm with practical gate opening
- Removable ceiling / storage divider: approx. 1080 × 860 mm
- Roof left slope: approx. 1200 × 600 mm
- Roof right slope: approx. 1200 × 600 mm

Gate opening:

- Width: approx. 700 mm
- Height: approx. 420 mm

The Gothic arch can be decorative and larger than the actual gate opening.

## One-sheet feasibility

A single 2500 × 1250 mm sheet can cover most of the minimal structural shell, but roof, false gable, tower base, shelves, and mistakes/offcuts may require either:

- a second sheet,
- smaller offcut panels,
- or using thinner/decorative material for non-structural Gothic elements.

The comfort variant will normally require more than one sheet.

## Recommended use of offcuts

Use offcuts for:

- Internal shelves.
- Cable cover.
- Power supply mounting panel.
- False front gable.
- Tower base.
- Erker backing plates.
- Decorative buttress backing plates.

## Assembly note

Use 30×30 or 40×40 mm battens as the main structural joining method. Do not rely on tight plywood slots unless using CNC.
