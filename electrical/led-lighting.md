# LED lighting

## Goal

Add warm decorative lighting without compromising robot safety or weather protection.

## Recommended lighting zones

1. Interior warm light above robot bay.
2. Backlight behind stained-glass windows.
3. Small tower light.
4. Optional low entrance glow.

## Power

- Use low-voltage 12 V LED lighting.
- Place LED power supply in the upper service compartment if it is ventilated.
- Use cable glands for exterior cable paths.
- Add strain relief.
- Keep all wiring outside the robot movement path.

## Installation notes

- Use LED strip channels or clips.
- Avoid loose adhesive-only mounting in warm outdoor conditions.
- Use diffusers behind stained-glass windows.
- Keep LEDs accessible through the removable roof.

## Safety checklist

- No cable below robot wheels.
- No exposed connector near floor water.
- Power supply ventilated.
- Roof access does not pull cables.
- Lighting can be disconnected independently.
